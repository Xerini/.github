## Who we are

Xerini is a London-based AI and data consultancy founded by three technologists with 50+ years of combined experience building mission-critical systems inside investment banks. We do not advise on software. We build it. When we say six weeks, we mean six weeks.

We work with organisations in the **built environment, engineering, finance, and asset management** sectors where the cost of bad data is measured in millions, not marketing slides.

## What we ship

### [Xefr](https://xefr.io) — the data orchestration platform

Everyone is building AI tools. Nobody is connecting them. Xefr is the glue that unifies data across your existing systems (APIs, databases, SaaS apps, file shares) into one governed, AI-searchable layer. No rip and replace. No 18-month migration. Live in weeks.

- Connect any system
- AI-powered search across every source
- White-label ready for partners
- ISO 27001 / 9001 certified, GDPR compliant
- Self-host, private cloud, or managed by us

**Built for the messy middle**: 50 to 500 people, a dozen systems that do not talk to each other, no appetite for a two-year transformation.
